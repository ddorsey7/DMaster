Star Shower

  • High Concept: Survive the star shower
  • Player Experience Goals: The experience I want the player to feel is fun with quick reflexes.
  • Genre: Casual game
  • Game play: Avoid getting hit by comets for 60 seconds
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanic this is prototype evaluating the movement with arrow keys.
  • Features: The unique aspects of my game that set it apart from my others is that I'm using key controls instead of a mouse.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a captain, guiding your ship and crew through the treacherous comets
  • Story: Your ship has entered a star shower and you must
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers.
  • Play time: One minute
  • Strategies: Use all four arrow keys to dodge comets.
  • Prototype Assets: Assets use were clip art of comets and rockets and a real picture of stars
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Games that most closely resembles my game are driving 2D games about dodging traffic.
