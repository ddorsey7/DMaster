The Interview

  • High Concept: Stop the North Korea bombs from falling!
  • Player Experience Goals: The experience I want the player to feel is fun and stress at the same time.
  • Genre: A casual game, like candy crush, with little strategy and more timing.
  • Game play: Catch the bombs before they fall for 60 seconds. You must have a 1000 points or more to win. For bomb you do not catch you'll lose points
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanic this is prototype evaluating the best movement and balance for difficulty.
  • Features: The unique aspects of my game that set it apart is it base on the movie the Interview.
  • Setting/Premise: Your an American jetfighter stopping North Korean attacks against the free nations
  • Story: Kim Jong has launch nukes after he failed the Interview. The U.S. has launch a jetfighter
  • Target Audience: Fans of the Interview.
  • Play time: One minute
  • Strategies: Start from the top to give you more time if you miss.
  • Prototype Assets: Assets use where a cat drawing, ball animation, power, and local phazer forum
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Games that most closely resembles my game are "Bounce and Knock", "accelerate to pointer", "Gravity", and "Group vs. Self."
