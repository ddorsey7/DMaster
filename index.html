Body Defense

  • High Concept: Kill the viruses before they kill you!
  • Player Experience Goals: The experience I want the player to feel is fun and stress at the same time.
  • Genre: A survival, action game, like Galaxy War.
  • Game play: Shoot all the virus before they get you until time runs out.
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanic this is prototype evaluating the point and shoot mechanic.
  • Features: The unique aspects of my game that set it apart is it base on immune system defending the body.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a lone white cell defending the body.
  • Story: The body is infected with a deadly virus, survive the infestation.
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers.
  • Play time: One minute
  • Strategies: Hold down the mouse and shoot the viruses closest to you.
  • Prototype Assets: Assets use where a cartoon drawings and a 3D picture of the bloodstream
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Games that most closely resembles my game are "Bounce and Knock", "accelerate to pointer", "point and shoot", and "Group vs. Self."
