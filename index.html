Bat Attack

  • High Concept: Throw batarangs at joker cards
  • Player Experience Goals: The experience I want the player to feel like batman playing a mini game
  • Genre: Casual game
  • Game play: Score as many cards as you can, but don't miss or you'll lose points
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanic this is prototype the launcher and physics mechanic
  • Features: The unique aspects of my game that set it apart from my others is that I'm using projectile physicals to throw the batarang
  • Setting/Premise: You are batman throwing batarangs at joker card, because YOU ARE BATMAN!
  • Story: No real story
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers and batman fans
  • Play time: Unlimited
  • Strategies: Time shots in path of cards
  • Prototype Assets: Assets used were the bat symbol, joker images, and the background from Arkam City.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I think of duck hunt as the closest comparison, with the moving targets and projectiles
